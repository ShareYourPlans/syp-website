Child Safety Standards

Last updated: April 2026  |  Share Your Plans SRL · BCE No. BE1034.288.729

Zero tolerance. Share Your Plans (SYP) has an absolute zero-tolerance policy for child sexual abuse and exploitation (CSAE) in any form. Any violation results in immediate account termination and reporting to the relevant authorities.

1. Age Requirement

SYP is strictly reserved for users aged 18 and older. We do not knowingly allow minors to register or use the platform. Age is verified at registration through date of birth. Accounts found to belong to individuals under 18 will be immediately suspended and permanently deleted.

2. Prohibited Content & Behavior

The following are strictly prohibited on SYP:

  • Any content that sexually exploits, endangers, or depicts minors
  • Any communication intended to groom, solicit, or harm minors
  • Any attempt to use the platform to engage in illegal activity involving children
  • Sharing, distributing, or requesting child sexual abuse material (CSAM)

3. Enforcement

SYP actively monitors the platform for violations of this policy. When a violation is detected or reported:

  • The account is immediately and permanently suspended
  • All relevant data is preserved and reported to law enforcement authorities
  • The incident is reported to the National Center for Missing & Exploited Children (NCMEC)
  • In Indonesia, incidents are also reported to Komnas Perlindungan Anak (National Commission for Child Protection) and Bareskrim Polri (Indonesian National Police Criminal Investigation Unit)

4. User Reporting

Every user has the ability to report suspicious content or behavior directly within the app using the built-in Report feature. All reports are reviewed promptly by our moderation team. Reports can also be sent directly to our safety team by email.
