Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy applies to the SYP mobile application as made available at the date of its initial launch in Indonesia.

1. Identity of the Data Controller

The entity responsible for processing your personal data in connection with the SYP application is:

Share Your Plans SRL
Incorporated under Belgian law  |  BCE No. BE1034.288.729
Email: [email protected]

Share Your Plans SRL acts as the sole data controller for all personal data collected through the Platform. There is no subsidiary or local entity involved in data processing at this stage of operations.

2. Scope of This Policy

This Privacy Policy applies to all Users who access or use the SYP mobile application within the territory of Indonesia. It describes how we collect, use, store, share, and protect your personal data, and how you may exercise your rights under applicable Indonesian law.

This Policy is governed by the Indonesian Personal Data Protection Law (UU PDP No. 27/2022), the Electronic Information and Transactions Law (UU ITE No. 11/2008 as amended), and Government Regulation No. 71/2019 on Electronic System and Transaction Operations (GR 71).

3. Personal Data We Collect

We collect only the personal data necessary to operate and deliver the Platform's features.

3.1 Account and Profile Data

When you register for a SYP account, we collect the following:

  • First name and last name (mandatory — only your first name is displayed to other users, and only after Double Acceptance of a Match)
  • Email address (mandatory to subscribe)
  • Date of birth (mandatory — used solely to verify the minimum age requirement of 18 years)
  • City of residence (mandatory — for internal statistical analysis; displayed on the user profile after Double Acceptance)
  • Country of residence (mandatory — to determine the applicable legal framework and segment users by market)
  • Preferred match distance and time margin (mandatory — used exclusively by the matching algorithm; never triggers device location permissions)
  • Language(s) spoken (mandatory — used to determine your app interface language)
  • Profile photo (mandatory — displayed to other users after Double Acceptance)
  • Nickname (optional — displayed in Explorer Mode only; must not contain any personally identifiable information)
  • Brief bio (optional — displayed on your profile after Double Acceptance)
  • Password (stored in hashed, non-reversible form — never accessible in plain text)

Profile information is visible to other users only after the Double Acceptance process is completed, except for nickname and activity address which are visible in Explorer Mode to Gold subscribers.

3.2 Plan Data

When you create or publish a Plan on the Platform, we collect: title of the activity, category (one of five fixed options: Sport, Culture, Food, Travel, Social), specific address of the planned activity as entered by you via Google Maps, date and time, and an optional description.

The activity address is a fixed, user-entered property of the Plan. It is never derived from your device location, and its collection does not constitute user geolocation in any form.

3.3 Location Data — Permanent Policy

SYP does not collect, and will never collect, any GPS coordinates or real-time device location data. This is a permanent architectural and product commitment. The application does not request, access, or process device location permissions at any point.

Location information in SYP is limited exclusively to two manually entered data points: the city and country you provide at registration, and the specific Google Maps address you enter when creating a Plan. No location data is ever automatically derived from your device.

3.4 Usage and Technical Data

We automatically collect: device type and operating system version, app version, session timestamps and duration, log data (errors, crash reports), and IP address (used for security and fraud prevention only — not used for location tracking).

3.5 Communication Data

Once a Match is finalised through the Double Acceptance process, Users may exchange messages through the Platform's in-app Chat Thread. We store the content of these messages to ensure the integrity and continuity of the service, and to enable content moderation where required.

3.6 Payment Data

If you subscribe to a payment plan:

  • On Android devices, payments are processed via Google Play Billing
  • On iOS devices, payments are processed via Apple StoreKit
  • For web or alternative payment flows (if applicable), we may use providers such as HitPay or Stripe, acting as independent data controllers

SYP does not store full payment card numbers or sensitive financial credentials. We retain only the data necessary to manage your subscription (subscription tier, billing status, transaction reference).

3.7 Inferences and Derived Data

As part of its Matching algorithm, the Platform generates inferences about your preferences and activity patterns based on the Plans you create and the Matches you accept or decline. These inferences are used solely to improve the relevance of future Match suggestions. They are not shared with third parties, not used for advertising purposes, and are permanently deleted upon account closure.

4. Legal Basis for Processing

Under UU PDP No. 27/2022, we process your personal data on the following legal bases:

  • Consent — for optional profile data. You may withdraw consent at any time.
  • Performance of a Contract — for account data, Plan data, and communication data necessary to provide the service.
  • Legitimate Interest — for technical and usage data to maintain security, stability, and performance.
  • Legal Obligation — where required by Indonesian law, including UU ITE and GR 71.

5. Purposes of Processing

We use your personal data strictly for the following purposes: creating and managing your user account and profile; operating the Plan publication and Matching features; delivering Match notifications and enabling in-app messaging after Double Acceptance; generating inferences to improve Match relevance; managing subscription status and billing records; ensuring platform security and preventing fraud; responding to user support requests; complying with legal obligations; and improving Platform performance through aggregated, anonymised analytics.

SYP does not use personal data for targeted advertising or cross-application tracking. We do not sell personal data to advertisers.

Users are solely responsible for their interactions and any in-person meetings with other users. SYP does not verify user identity beyond basic registration requirements and does not guarantee the behaviour of any user.

6. Data Storage and Infrastructure

6.1 Storage Location

All personal data is stored on Railway infrastructure (application hosting) and Neon (PostgreSQL database), both operating on cloud infrastructure with data processing in accordance with applicable data protection standards.

In addition, we use selected third-party service providers strictly necessary for the operation of the Platform, including:

  • Google Firebase — push notifications, analytics, crash reporting
  • Google Play Billing — subscription management and in-app purchases (Android)
  • Apple StoreKit — subscription management and in-app purchases (iOS)
  • Apple Push Notification Service (APNs) — push notifications (iOS)

These providers process data under their own privacy policies and in accordance with applicable regulations.

6.2 Security Measures

We implement appropriate technical and organisational measures to protect your personal data, including: encryption of data in transit (TLS 1.2 or higher), encryption of data at rest (Neon PostgreSQL), access controls and role-based permissions, authentication through Keycloak including token-based session management, and rate limiting to mitigate abuse.

6.3 Data Retention

  • Account and profile data: retained for the duration of the account and deleted within 30 days of a valid deletion request
  • Plan data and Match history: retained for the duration of the account
  • In-app Chat Thread messages: retained for the duration of the account
  • Inferred data: deleted upon account closure
  • Technical logs: retained for a maximum of 90 days
  • Billing records: retained for 5 years in accordance with Indonesian accounting and tax obligations

6.4 Inactive Accounts

If your account has been inactive for a continuous period of 2 years (no login or activity), we reserve the right to close it automatically. You will be notified by email at least 30 days before any automatic closure.

6.5 Post-Closure Safety Retention Window

Following account closure, we retain a limited subset of your data for 3 months as a safety retention window (extended to 1 year in the event of an account ban). The purpose is to enable investigation of any unlawful conduct or violations that may come to our attention after closure.

6.6 Data Breach Notification

In the event of a personal data breach that is likely to affect your rights or security, we will notify affected users and relevant authorities in accordance with applicable laws, including UU PDP No. 27/2022.

7. Sharing of Personal Data

We do not sell, rent, or commercially exploit your personal data. We share personal data only in the following limited circumstances:

  • With Other Users — certain profile information is visible after Double Acceptance. In Explorer Mode, only nickname and Plan details are visible.
  • User Reports and Moderation — data may be reviewed internally to ensure platform safety.
  • Infrastructure Providers (Railway, Neon) — infrastructure data for application hosting and database storage.
  • Technology Providers (Google Firebase) — device and usage data for app functionality, analytics, and crash reporting.
  • Payment Providers (Google Play Billing, Apple StoreKit) — subscription status and billing processing.
  • With Authorities — where required by Indonesian law, court order, or governmental authority.
  • In Corporate Restructuring — in the event of a merger or acquisition, with equivalent privacy protections and prior user notification.

We only share the minimum data necessary for each purpose.

8. Your Rights Under UU PDP

As a data subject under UU PDP No. 27/2022, you have the right to: access your data, correct inaccuracies, request deletion (fulfilled within 30 days), restrict processing, object to processing based on legitimate interest, withdraw consent, and request data portability.

To exercise your rights, submit a written request to [email protected]. We will respond within 14 calendar days. You also have the right to lodge a complaint with the competent Indonesian supervisory authority responsible for personal data protection.

9. Protection of Minors
